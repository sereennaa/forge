Structural Engineering Services

Structural engineering support for renovations, additions, assessments, and new construction. We focus on clear drawings, practical details, and responsive coordination from early design through permit and construction.

Residential

Structural design for homes, additions, renovations, and small residential buildings. We work with homeowners, architects, and contractors to develop practical solutions for existing conditions, permit submissions, and construction.

  • Renovations and additions
  • Load-bearing wall removals and new openings
  • Foundations, underpinning, and structural repairs
  • New homes, laneway suites, and small multi-unit buildings
  • Permit drawings and construction review support

Institutional, Commercial & Industrial (ICI)

Structural engineering for building upgrades, fit-outs, alterations, and additions in institutional, commercial, and light industrial settings. We provide clear documentation and coordinate closely with the project team.

  • School, office, retail, and light industrial projects
  • Interior alterations and structural modifications
  • Rooftop equipment, openings, and local reinforcement
  • Small additions and building upgrades
  • Coordination with architects, contractors, and consultants

Assessments & Reports

Structural reviews and written reports for existing buildings, renovations, property reviews, and damage concerns. We provide clear observations, practical recommendations, and appropriate limitations based on the scope of review.

  • Structural condition assessments
  • Pre-renovation and pre-purchase reviews
  • Damage reviews and repair recommendations
  • Site observation reports
  • Support for insurance, legal, or due diligence matters

Why Forge Structural

Practical Structural
Engineering

Forge Structural provides structural engineering for residential, commercial, institutional, and industrial projects. We focus on practical solutions that support efficient construction, clear coordination, and straightforward permit reviews.

Our work includes renovations, additions, assessments, structural modifications, and new construction. We value clear coordination with owners, architects, consultants, and contractors so structural decisions stay aligned with the broader project.

Forge Structural is led by Elio Haddad, P.Eng., an Oakville-based structural engineer with 14 years of experience across residential, commercial, institutional, and industrial projects in Ontario. His background includes renovations, retrofits, new construction, construction review, and close coordination with owners, architects, consultants, and contractors. Forge Structural was created to offer practical structural engineering with clear communication, a collaborative approach, and hands-on support through design and construction.

Our Process

How We Work Together

A practical process for moving from initial inquiry to permit, construction, or reporting, with the level of support tailored to the project.

01

Consultation

Share your project details, drawings, photos, and timeline. Simple scopes can often be priced quickly. Larger projects may require a more detailed review.

02

Site Review

We review the site or available drawings to understand existing conditions and identify structural considerations early.

03

Design

We develop practical structural solutions and prepare P.Eng. stamped drawings suited to the project scope.

04

Permit Support

We help respond to municipal or consultant comments so the structural review can move forward clearly.

05

Construction Review

We provide field review and support during construction to address questions and help confirm general conformance with the design intent.
